Past Master’s Night

Honoring Leadership, Legacy & Service

Spring 2026 • Date to be Announced

Each spring, Shidle Lodge No. 601 gathers for a special evening dedicated to honoring our Past Masters—both living and deceased—whose leadership, guidance, and commitment have shaped our Lodge across generations.

50-Year Service Pin Ceremony

During the evening’s ceremony, we proudly recognize brethren who have reached the remarkable milestone of 50 years of Masonic service. Family members and close friends are encouraged to attend and share in this meaningful celebration.
