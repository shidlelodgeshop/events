Past Master's Night

Honoring Leadership, Legacy, and Service

Spring 2026 • Date to be Announced

Each spring, Shidle Lodge No. 601 gathers to honor our Past Masters—both living and deceased—whose leadership and dedication have shaped our Lodge. The evening includes a dinner prior to our stated meeting and a formal recognition ceremony.

50-Year Service Pin Ceremony

We proudly present the 50-Year Service Pins to brethren who have reached this milestone. Family and friends of honorees are warmly welcomed to attend.
